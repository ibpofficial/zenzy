Stay Safe with Zenzy

Your safety is our priority — here's how to protect yourself

🛡️ Before Booking

  • Always book through the official Zenzy platform
  • Check provider ratings, reviews & verification badges
  • Review service description, pricing & included items
  • Ensure your address & contact details are accurate

🏠 During Service

  • Verify provider identity with the OTP code
  • Never share financial information (bank details, PINs)
  • Keep a friend or family member informed
  • Use in-app chat for all communication

💳 Payment Safety

  • Always pay through the Zenzy platform
  • Never transfer money to a personal account
  • Verify the final amount before confirming payment
  • Save payment receipts & transaction IDs

🏘️ Rental Safety

  • Visit the property in person before any payment
  • Verify landlord identity & ownership documents
  • Never pay deposits outside the Zenzy platform
  • Document property condition with photos

✅ Quick Safety Tips

📱 Book via app 🔐 Verify with OTP 💬 Use in-app chat ⭐ Rate every service 🚫 Never share OTPs 📞 Contact support 📸 Document everything 📍 Share location

👷 For Service Providers

  • Always carry your Zenzy ID & verification documents
  • Confirm booking details & OTP with the customer
  • Maintain professional conduct & respect customer property
  • Report any unsafe situations through the app
  • Do not accept or request payments outside the platform

🔄 Additional Safety Practices

📸 Take before/after photos 📍 Share your live location 🔔 Enable notifications 🧾 Keep records of communication 🚪 Set clear work boundaries 📋 Review cancellation policy

🚨 Emergency & Reporting

If you encounter a safety issue or emergency:

  • In-App SOS: Use the emergency button for immediate assistance
  • Zenzy Support: Contact our 24/7 support team through the Help Desk
🚔 Police: 112 👩 Women's Helpline: 1091 🧒 Child Helpline: 1098

All safety reports are treated with the highest priority and confidentiality.
