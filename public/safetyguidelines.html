Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines when using Zenzy's services.

🛡️ Before Booking

Take these precautions before confirming a service booking:

  • Always book through the official Zenzy platform — never via personal contacts shared by providers
  • Check the service provider's ratings, reviews, and verification badges
  • Review the service description, pricing, and included items carefully
  • Ensure your address and contact details are accurate for the booking
  • For high-value services, consider being present or having someone you trust at the location

🏠 During Service

Stay safe while the service is being performed at your location:

  • Verify the provider's identity using the OTP code generated through the app
  • Never share personal financial information (bank details, passwords, PINs) with providers
  • Keep a family member or friend informed about the service appointment
  • Do not leave valuables unattended in the work area
  • Monitor the work being performed — ask questions if something seems unusual
  • Use the in-app chat for all communication to maintain a record

💳 Payment Safety

Protect yourself during transactions:

  • Always pay through the Zenzy platform — avoid cash payments when possible
  • Never transfer money directly to a provider's personal account
  • Verify the final amount matches what was quoted before confirming payment
  • Save payment receipts and transaction IDs for your records
  • Report any requests for additional payments outside the platform immediately

🏘️ Rental Property Safety

If you're using Zenzy's rental marketplace:

  • Always visit the property in person before making any payment or commitment
  • Verify the landlord's identity and ownership documents through official channels
  • Never pay deposits or advance rent outside the Zenzy platform
  • Check the neighborhood, connectivity, and safety of the area
  • Read and understand the lease agreement thoroughly before signing
  • Document the property condition with photos during move-in

👷 For Service Providers

If you're a registered service professional on Zenzy:

  • Always carry your Zenzy ID and verification documents during service calls
  • Confirm the booking details and OTP with the customer before starting work
  • Maintain professional conduct and respect the customer's property and privacy
  • Report any unsafe situations or customer misconduct through the app
  • Do not accept or request payments outside the Zenzy platform

🚨 Emergency & Reporting

If you encounter a safety issue or emergency:

  • In-App SOS: Use the emergency button in the app for immediate assistance
  • Zenzy Support: Contact our 24/7 support team through the Help Desk
  • Police Emergency: Dial 112 for immediate police assistance
  • Women's Helpline: Dial 1091 for women's safety concerns

All safety reports are treated with the highest priority and confidentiality. We take every report seriously and investigate thoroughly.
