Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025 · How Zenzy uses cookies to enhance your experience.

🍪 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our platform. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our services, and improve your overall experience. Cookies do not contain personal information like passwords or payment details.

📋 Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Required
Essential Authentication, security, basic platform functionality Session Required
Functional Language preferences, location settings, theme choices 1 year Required
Analytics Usage patterns, page views, feature adoption metrics 2 years Optional
Marketing Personalized recommendations and relevant promotions 90 days Optional

🔒 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the platform to function. They enable core features such as:

  • Keeping you logged in during your browsing session
  • Maintaining your shopping cart and booking selections
  • Ensuring secure transactions and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing and server optimization

These cookies cannot be disabled as they are fundamental to the operation of Zenzy.

📊 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our platform. This data helps us improve our services, identify popular features, and fix issues. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized — we never use it to personally identify individual users.

🎯 Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies help us deliver relevant service recommendations and promotions. These cookies track your browsing patterns across our platform to suggest services you might find useful. You can opt out of marketing cookies at any time without affecting your access to the platform.

⚙️ Managing Your Cookies

You have full control over cookie preferences:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings menu
  • Platform Settings: Visit your Zenzy account settings to manage cookie consent preferences
  • Opt-Out Links: For analytics, you can use the opt-out mechanisms provided by our analytics partners

Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent certain platform features from functioning properly.

🤝 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our platform are placed by trusted third-party services that we use for analytics, payment processing, and customer support. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the use of cookies. We only partner with services that meet our data protection standards.

🔄 Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will notify you through the platform. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.
